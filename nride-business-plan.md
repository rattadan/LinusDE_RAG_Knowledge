### Revenue Streams

1. **Protocol Fees** (70% of revenue)
   - 1.5% on all rides
   - $0.0001 per reputation query

2. **Enterprise Licensing** (25%)
   - $5K/month per operator
   - Custom integration fees

3. **Data Marketplace** (5%)
   - Anonymized mobility patterns
   - Dynamic pricing models
